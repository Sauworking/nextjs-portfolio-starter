#SUTRA NETI - Yoga Cleansing Technique

Sutra Neti, is a Hatha yoga technique to clean your nasal cavity. Sutra Neti is one of the six methods of purification.
There are various types of Neti practiced by Yogis including Jala Neti (with water), Dughda Neti (with Milk), Sutra Neti (with string), etc. In Sutra Neti, a waxed cotton string/ or rubber tube is used to clean the nasal cavity.
